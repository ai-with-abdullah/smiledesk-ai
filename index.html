FAQ

Questions dentists ask us

Will patients know they're talking to an AI?

The agent introduces itself as the clinic's virtual assistant and speaks naturally. Patients care about getting an answer and an appointment fast — that's exactly what it delivers. Any call it can't handle is transferred to your staff.

What happens with real emergencies?

Emergencies are detected and immediately forwarded to your front desk or on-call number, with a summary of what the patient said. The AI never "handles" an emergency alone.

Do I need to change my phone number or software?

No. We forward your existing number's unanswered/after-hours calls to the agent, and bookings go into the calendar you already use. Zero disruption to your current setup.

How long does setup take?

One 15-minute call with you, then we go live within 48 hours. We do all the technical work.

What if it makes a mistake?

Every call is recorded and transcribed, so you can review anything. During your free pilot we monitor calls daily and tune the agent for your clinic. You approve everything before we go live.

Why is the pilot free?

Because the results sell themselves. After 14 days you'll see exactly how many calls were answered and appointments booked. If the numbers don't make sense for your clinic, you pay nothing.
