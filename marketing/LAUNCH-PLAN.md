## The Offer (memorize this)

> "I install a 24/7 AI receptionist on your clinic's phone line, completely free for 14 days.
> It answers every call and books appointments while your staff is busy or the clinic is closed.
> After 14 days I show you exactly how many calls it answered and appointments it booked.
> If you like the numbers, it's $249/month. If not, you pay nothing and keep the report."

Why this works: zero risk for the dentist, and the results report closes the sale for you.

## Conversion Math (why this works)

- 100 cold contacts → ~5–10 replies → 3–5 demos → 1–2 free pilots
- A free pilot that answers even 15 missed calls and books 5 appointments sells itself.
- 2 paying clinics = ~$500/month recurring. 10 clinics = $2,500/month. 20 = $5,000/month.

## Pilot Delivery (keep pilot costs at $0)

Run pilots on free tiers so you spend nothing before revenue:
- **Voice agent:** Vapi / Retell / Bland free trial credits, or your existing n8n setup (you have n8n-local).
- **Calendar:** the clinic's existing Google Calendar (free).
- **Call forwarding:** the clinic sets "forward when unanswered" on their own line — costs them nothing.
- **Limit the pilot:** after-hours + missed-call overflow only. Caps usage so free credits last 14 days.

## Golden rules

- Never pause outreach. 20 contacts every weekday, no exceptions — pipelines die in silence.
- Sell the RESULT (booked patients), never the technology (AI, LLMs, agents).
- Every pilot ends with a results report + a clear ask: "Should we keep it running at $249/month?"
- Get a testimonial from every happy client before you celebrate.
