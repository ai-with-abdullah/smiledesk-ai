# SmileDesk AI — Outreach Scripts (copy, personalize, send)

**Rule for every message:** change the first line to something specific about THAT clinic
(their reviews, location, website, or the fact they didn't answer when you called).
Generic blasts get ignored; personalized ones get replies.

## Objection handling (for demo calls)

**"How much does it cost?"**
→ "The 14-day pilot is completely free — I even do the setup. After that it's $249/month,
which is usually less than the value of ONE recovered patient. And you'll have a report
showing exactly what it earned you before you decide."

**"Patients won't like talking to a robot."**
→ "Fair concern — listen to the demo, most patients can't tell and honestly don't care;
they want an answer and an appointment NOW. And compare it to the alternative: today
those patients hear ringing and call your competitor."

**"We already have a receptionist."**
→ "Perfect — this isn't a replacement, it's backup. It only takes the calls she physically
can't: when she's with a patient, at lunch, after 6 PM, weekends. Those are exactly the
calls you're losing now."

**"I need to think about it."**
→ "Of course. That's exactly why the pilot is free — you don't have to decide anything today.
Give me 15 minutes for setup info, and in 14 days you decide with real numbers instead
of guessing. What's a bad outcome here?"

**"Is my patient data safe?"**
→ "Calls are recorded and transcribed only for your own review, bookings go into your
own calendar, and nothing is shared with anyone. You approve everything before we go live,
and we sign a simple agreement if you'd like."

**"We don't miss many calls."**
→ "Might be true! Easy test: I'll check your line for a week during the free pilot and
show you the real number. If you truly miss almost nothing, the report is free and
I'll tell you honestly you don't need me."
